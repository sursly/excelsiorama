Use the type testers above to explore Excelsiorama. While it is a work in progress, it is ready for a variety of wacky uses. The core set of letters, numbers, and punctuation were completed by over 30 collaborators across Upstate NY. Alternates continue to trickle in, adding much appreciated depth to the display face. Contributors snagged glyphs on a first come first served basis, with zero direction and guidance. Excelsiorama is free and open sourced on Github. We'd love to see what you do with it (send a tweet to @AIGAUPSTNY or @adventureson.
